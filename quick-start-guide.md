# Quick Start Guide

## Welcome

This guide helps you get started with MyApp in just a few minutes.

## Step 1: Create an Account

1. Open MyApp.
2. Click **Sign Up**.
3. Enter your email and password.
4. Click **Create Account**.

## Step 2: Create Your First Task

1. Click **New Task**.
2. Enter a task title.
3. Add a description.
4. Click **Save**.

## Step 3: Set a Due Date

1. Open the task.
2. Select **Due Date**.
3. Choose a date and save.

## Step 4: Track Progress

Use the task status options:

* To Do
* In Progress
* Completed

## Next Steps

Explore notifications, reminders, and task categories to get the most out of MyApp.
