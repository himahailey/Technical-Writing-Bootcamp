# Notion User Guide: Getting Started

## Purpose
This guide helps new team members set up and use Notion for documentation, project tracking, and collaboration.

## Audience
New employees with no prior Notion experience.

## Prerequisites
- Company email account
- Internet connection
- Invitation to the workspace

## What is Notion?
Notion is a workspace used to create documents, manage projects, maintain knowledge bases, and collaborate with team members.

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Join the Workspace
1. Open the invitation email.
2. Click **Join Workspace**.
3. Sign in using your company email.
4. Complete profile setup.

### Step 2: Create Your First Page
1. Click **New Page**.
2. Enter a page title.
3. Add content using text blocks.
4. Save changes automatically.

### Step 3: Use Templates
1. Click **Templates**.
2. Select a template category.
3. Choose a template.
4. Customize it for your needs.

### Step 4: Share a Page
1. Open the page.
2. Click **Share**.
3. Enter team member email addresses.
4. Choose permissions.
5. Click **Invite**.

## Best Practices
- Use clear page titles.
- Organize content with headings.
- Keep related information together.
- Archive outdated pages regularly.

## Troubleshooting

### Cannot Access Workspace
- Verify your email address.
- Check internet connectivity.
- Contact your administrator.

### Unable to Share Pages
- Confirm you have sharing permissions.
- Refresh the page and try again.
