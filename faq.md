# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## What is MyApp?

MyApp is a productivity application designed to help users manage tasks and projects efficiently.

## How do I reset my password?

1. Click **Forgot Password** on the login page.
2. Enter your registered email address.
3. Follow the instructions sent to your email.

## Can I use MyApp on mobile devices?

Yes. MyApp is available on both Android and iOS devices.

## How do I contact support?

You can contact support through the Help Center or by emailing [support@myapp.com](mailto:support@myapp.com).

## Is my data secure?

Yes. MyApp uses industry-standard security practices to protect user information.

## How do I update the application?

Updates are automatically installed when available. You can also check for updates manually in the Settings menu.
